Are you a witness ?

  If you witness school harassment, it is crucial to act promptly and it is totally understandable to feel upset and overwhelmed. Your courage in coming forward is commendable. Remember you are not alone.


Here what you can do :
  • Intervene :

    •   Calmly and assertively address the situation, telling the bully to stop and emphasizing the importance of treating others with respect.

  • Report:

    •   Inform a teacher, school staff, or any authority figure about the bullying incident. Provide details to help them understand the situation.

  • Support the victim:

    •   Offer comfort and support to the person being bullied. Let them know they're not alone and encourage them to report the incident as well.

  • Promote empathy:

    •   Encourage a positive and inclusive atmosphere by fostering empathy and kindness among students. Highlight the importance of understanding and respecting each other's differences.

  • Document incidents:

    •   Keep a record of any witnessed bullying incidents, including dates, times, and details. This information may be helpful when reporting the issue to school authorities or to the green number 2511
